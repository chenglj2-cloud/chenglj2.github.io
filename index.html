Five Ways to Eat a Chicken

A zero-waste kitchen guide for novices living alone

Author: Jeanne Jiang

The Chicken Breast Tragedy

When I first started living alone, I bought chicken breast, broccoli and potatoes thinking I would become the kind of adult who meal-preps every weekend.

Day 1: grilled chicken breast + broccoli + mashed potatoes.

Day 2: grilled chicken breast + broccoli + mashed potatoes.

Day 3: grilled chicken breast + broccoli + mashed potatoes.

By day four I stared at the fridge and wondered if this was what adulthood would always feel like.

That's when I discovered the secret: one ingredient can become many meals.

---
Recipe 1: Shredded Chicken Salad
Time: 10 minutes • Cost: ~$2.50
Ingredients
  • Shredded chicken
  • Lettuce
  • Shredded carrots
  • Salad dressing
Steps
  1. Tear lettuce into small pieces.
  2. Add shredded chicken and carrots.
  3. Drizzle dressing and mix.
  4. Serve directly.
---
Recipe 2: Chicken Rice Bowl
Time: 15 minutes • Cost: ~$1.80
Ingredients
  • Shredded chicken
  • Rice
  • Ginger slices
  • Salt & white pepper
  • Egg (optional)
Steps
  1. Boil water with ginger slices.
  2. Add rice and shredded chicken.
  3. Cook for 3–5 minutes.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
---
Recipe 3: Hand-Torn Chicken Legs
Time: 15 minutes • Cost: ~$2.00
Ingredients
  • Chicken legs
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Chili oil (optional)
Steps
  1. Heat the chicken legs.
  2. Mix garlic, soy sauce and sesame oil.
  3. Tear the chicken legs into pieces.
  4. Drizzle sauce and serve.
---
Recipe 4: Chicken Egg Fried Rice
Time: 12 minutes • Cost: ~$2.20
Ingredients
  • Rice
  • Shredded chicken
  • 1 egg
  • Diced carrots
  • Diced onion
  • Soy sauce
Steps
  1. Scramble the egg and set aside.
  2. Stir fry onion and carrots.
  3. Add rice and break apart.
  4. Add chicken and egg.
  5. Add soy sauce and stir fry.
---
Recipe 5: Chicken Soup Noodles
Time: 20 minutes • Cost: ~$1.50
Ingredients
  • Chicken soup base
  • Noodles
  • Shredded chicken
  • Greens
  • Ginger slices
Steps
  1. Boil chicken soup base.
  2. Add noodles and cook.
  3. Add chicken and greens.
  4. Season and serve.
